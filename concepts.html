04 Course Concepts

Three Frameworks

Three ideas that explain how fitness creators build trust and why audiences believe them. Each framework applied to all four creators.

01
Source Credibility Theory

We judge trust on three things. Expertise asks if they seem knowledgeable. Trustworthiness asks if they seem honest. Appeal asks if we like them. None of these require the person to actually be correct.

02
Parasocial Interaction

Viewers feel they know the creator personally. That feeling makes audiences accept recommendations as if they came from a trusted friend rather than a stranger online.

03
Healthism

Health as a personal moral duty. All four creators reinforce this in different styles. The styles look like opposites. The underlying message is the same.
