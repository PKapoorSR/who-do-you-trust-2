Four Findings

1
Looking credible and being credible are different things

Style and editing shape your opinion before you hear a single claim.

2
Raw honesty is still a strategy

Togi's overdose disclosures are as calculated as Jeff's citations. Transparency is a choice.

3
The most followed creator is not the most credible

Jeff scores highest on credibility. Togi scores lowest and has the more loyal audience. That gap is the point.

4
All four are selling the same idea

Science, comedy, honesty or spirituality. The message is always the same. Your body is your responsibility and I can help you fix it.
