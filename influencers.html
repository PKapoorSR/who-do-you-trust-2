Creator 03

Bro Science

Mike Tornabene as Dom Mazzetti @BroScienceLife on YouTube

Mike Tornabene is a comedian and graduate of New York University. He created the character Dom Mazzetti in 2012. The character is a parody of gym culture and gives exaggerated or intentionally incorrect fitness advice for comedy. The channel became popular because many jokes are based on real gym stereotypes and behaviors. Mike Tornabene also released a book called The Swoly Bible in 2016 and has worked with Seven Bucks Productions connected to Dwayne Johnson.

Creator 04

More Plates More Dates

Derek @moreplatesmoredates on YouTube

Derek launched More Plates More Dates in 2016 as a bodybuilding channel that gradually expanded into steroid use, hormone therapy, and supplement research. He does not hold a medical degree or scientific certification. His audience grew through the level of detail in his videos and his willingness to cover topics that many fitness creators do not address. He is the founder of Gorilla Mind, a supplement company, and a co-founder of Marek Health, a hormone optimization clinic. Both businesses are related to topics he regularly covers on the channel.
